Our Fantasy Football League

Basics

  • League name: HFFFL
  • 12 participants: referred to as GMs (general managers)
  • Draft: Auction style, $100
  • Dynasty League: our rosters don’t reset each year. Each GM keeps the same number of players on his roster every year, after the 2019 season that was 3 keepers per team.
  • Keepers: 3 per team (high, mid, low). High keepers were players drafted for $15+. Mid keepers were drafted for $5-$15. Low keepers were drafted for $4 or less. You can categorize keepers up, but not down. So a GM could retain 3 “low” players from one season to the next, but each would be assigned a designation of high, mid, or low.
